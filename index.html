Anurag Charitable Trust is a registered Non-Governmental Organization established on 12th September 2017, located at Yavat, Daund subdivision of Pune, Maharashtra (also trying to spread helping hands all over India) The Vision of the Organization is to create a resourceful environment with an awareness of Menstrual Hygiene, setting up an extraordinary curriculum for underprivileged students, providing empowerment for the needy, standing by for environmental protection, and making a better place for animals (animal care centers). The current Missions of the Organization are: to help children to get basic education facilities, Taking care of orphan children to get the same basic education facilities, step towards eradication of the stigma, taboo, superstitions attached to Menstrual hygiene, and Mental Health; Protection of the environment and animals.
● Every out-of-school child should be able to attend the school, get all the facilities.
● Orphan children should get similar facilities like other children of the society.
● Complete eradication of the Taboo, Stigma and Superstitions attached to menstruations. Complete awareness about Menstrual Hygiene Management.
● Awareness about sustainable development, environment protection.
● Provide help to needy people and make them capable enough to earn for their living and live a happy life.
● Protection of animals and become the voice of voiceless.
● In the coming years, creating awareness about Menstrual Hygiene Management all over India.
● Enrollment of students who are working at Sugar factories, Brick factory, Jaggery factory.
● Tree plantations at every possible place.
● Official Animal Care Center.
● School with extra-ordinary curriculum with syllabus that teaches life skills.
